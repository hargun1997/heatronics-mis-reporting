**Purpose:** Track these two items on their own lines, separate from operating marketing/opex, across months.

## Treatment

- **Brand Building Investment** — long-term brand spend (agency/content), shown **between CM2 and CM3** (not mixed into performance-marketing CM2).
- **Cost of Fundraising** — one-time capital-raising cost, kept **separate below EBITDA** (out of operating results).
